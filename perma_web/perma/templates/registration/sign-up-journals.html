Perma.cc for journals

Journal editors care about the accuracy, integrity and reliability of citations. When web citations break, or the referenced content changes, that reflects poorly on the journal and the editors who work so hard to verify sources.

How journals use Perma.cc

Almost 300 journals use Perma to prevent link rot in their articles. Each journal receives a free organizational account and can designate editors to create permanent links for their web citations. Check out our user guide to learn more.
