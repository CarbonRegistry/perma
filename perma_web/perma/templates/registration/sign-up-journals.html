Perma.cc for Journals

Over 150 academic law journals prevent link rot with Perma.cc. The Bluebook now encourages archiving online sources when a reliable service such as Perma.cc is available.

Perma.cc is free for any academic journal that is authorized by its library. If your library is already registered with us (see the full list here), contact them for setup. If not, invite your library to visit our library resource page to request membership, and we'll help them get started.

In the meantime, our user guide has more info on how Perma.cc works for journals. And you can get started with a free individual account by completing this form.
