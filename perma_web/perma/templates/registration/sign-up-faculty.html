Perma.cc for faculty

Faculty use Perma.cc to prevent link rot in their scholarship. Faculty also can authorize others, such as assistants and researchers, to preserve web sources on their behalf.

Unlimited faculty accounts are free for any faculty member who is authorized by his or her library. If your library is already registered with us (see the full list here), contact them for setup. If not, invite your library to visit our library resource page to request membership, and we'll help them get started.

In the meantime, you can get started now by completing this form to create an individual account.
