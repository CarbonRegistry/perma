Perma.cc for faculty

Scholarship depends on accurate, reliable citations to stable sources. When web citations break, or the referenced content changes, that undermines good scholarship.

How faculty use Perma.cc

Individual faculty members can use Perma to prevent link rot in their scholarship. Faculty members can authorize others, such as assistants and researchers, to preserve web sources on their behalf. Please review our user guide to learn more about how Perma works for faculty.
