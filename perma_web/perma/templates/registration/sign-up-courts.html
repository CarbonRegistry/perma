Perma.cc for courts

Courts care deeply about the long-term accuracy, integrity and reliability of the citations in their opinions. When web citations break, or the referenced content changes, that undermines all the hard work put in to get citations right.

How courts use Perma.cc

Many court systems use Perma to prevent link rot in their opinions. Each court receives a free organizational account and can designate editors, clerks, reporters of decisions or others to create permanent links for their web citations. Check out our user guide to learn more.
