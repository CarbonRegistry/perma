About link rot

Websites change, go away, and get taken down. When they do, linked citations can lead to broken, blank, unintentional, or even malicious pages. This decay is called “link rot.”

Library-strength reliability

Perma.cc was built by Harvard’s Library Innovation Lab and is backed by the power of libraries. We’re both in the forever business: libraries already look after physical and digital materials — now we can do the same for links.

If you’re a librarian or are affiliated with a library, we can make using Perma.cc free and simple for you and the scholars that depend on you. We’ve answered many common questions and prepared a special library signup at the link below.
